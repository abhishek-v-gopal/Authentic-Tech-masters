About Us

The hackathon is an event usually meant to cracking solutions for real life problems with in a particular amount of time. It is a great time for students, tinkerers, engineers and all learning enthusiast to participate in a hackathon for solving challenging problems and make better solutions.
These hackathons provide a platform for networking, learning, and showcasing talent, and they may be sponsored or supported by government entities, educational institutions, tech companies, or other organizations interested in promoting innovation and collaboration within a specific state or region. The outcomes of these hackathons can include potential solutions to real-world problems, new business ideas, or simply a demonstration of the participants' technical skills and creativity.
